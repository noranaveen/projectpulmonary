Protecting the lungs of those who protect us.

Project Pulmonary is a registered 501(c)(3) environmental justice nonprofit supporting firefighters exposed to airborne carcinogens and toxic particulates.

Our Impact Model

The Issue

Firefighters face repeated exposure to toxic smoke and carcinogenic airborne compounds. Long-term respiratory consequences often go unrecognized.
