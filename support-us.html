Support Us

Support the mission behind every chapter, drive, and station partnership.

Donations help Project Pulmonary grow its chapter network, fund community outreach, and expand the practical work behind hydration drives, educational programming, and firefighter-centered support.

Contact for Giving Options Open Linktree
Where support goes

Chapter growth

Donations make it easier to support new school chapters with materials, outreach, and the operational basics needed to keep programs active.

Where support goes

Community events

Public events and outreach tables help Project Pulmonary explain its mission, meet local supporters, and bring pulmonary health into public conversation.

Where support goes

Service initiatives

Hydration drives, educational campaigns, and classroom-based activities all benefit from funding that keeps materials and project planning accessible.

Ways to support

Give, share, sponsor, or connect us to new communities.

  • Make a donation or ask about sponsoring a chapter or local event.
  • Share Project Pulmonary on social media and direct people to the Linktree.
  • Introduce the nonprofit to schools, organizations, or community partners.
  • Help strengthen the visibility of firefighter-centered pulmonary support.
