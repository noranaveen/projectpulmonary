Impact

Measurable support backed by actual chapters, real classrooms, and local station outreach.

Project Pulmonary’s impact is both quantitative and visible: supplies delivered, letters written, chapters formed, and conversations started.

11K+Hydration items donated to individual fire stations throughout the nation.
3K+Letters created and donated to firefighters across Project Pulmonary chapters.
30+Chapters expanding the nonprofit’s reach through school-based service.
What those numbers mean

Hydration Drives

These projects mobilize high school communities to collect useful items that can be directly delivered to firefighters. The format is easy to replicate, easy to scale, and immediately visible to the people receiving support.

What those numbers mean

Letters for Lungs

Letters connect elementary classrooms to the mission and bring encouragement into the nonprofit’s work. They also create an early awareness pathway for students who are just beginning to learn about public service.

What those numbers mean

Chapters

Every new chapter increases capacity for local outreach, school events, station partnerships, and leadership opportunities that keep the mission active in different communities.

Future direction

More chapters, more local partnerships, and broader awareness around firefighter lung health.

The growth path is straightforward: expand chapter presence, deepen school partnerships, and keep building a recognizable service model around pulmonary health.
