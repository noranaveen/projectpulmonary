About

Built around pulmonary health, community service, and youth-led follow-through.

Project Pulmonary is a nonprofit focused on the long-term respiratory risks firefighters face and the practical, visible service opportunities students can lead in response.

Who we are

A nonprofit that turns awareness into school-based action.

Project Pulmonary works at the intersection of pulmonary health, service, advocacy, and chapter-based leadership. We create approachable ways for students to engage in a serious issue through organized drives, educational outreach, and community events.

Why firefighters

They face repeated airborne exposure in the line of duty.

Smoke, toxic particulates, and carcinogens are part of the occupational reality firefighters navigate. Project Pulmonary is built to keep those long-term respiratory risks visible and actionable.

How we work

We build partnerships through local schools and community presence.

Our chapters organize projects with high schools and elementary schools, connect with local stations, and create service opportunities that translate directly into firefighter support.

What makes it distinct

Clear programs, visible outcomes, and a focused public-health mission.

Project Pulmonary is not a vague awareness campaign. It is a structured nonprofit with recognizable initiatives, measurable results, and a defined community-health purpose.
